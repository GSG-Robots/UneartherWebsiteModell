The GSG Robots

Hello, we are the GSG Robots from Ludwigshafen am Rhein the ugliest city germany and this is our research project: An autonomous excavation robot called "Unearther".

The Unearther

With the help of archaeologist, mechanists and ai-experts we managed to built this robot. It works similiar to a 3D-Printer, just the printing-bed was removed. The Unearther digs layer by layer trough the earth. It uses a shipscrew for digging and a vacuum cleaner to suck up the earth. To make it work it we used 7 Stepper Motor, each with it's own driver, a lot of 3D-printed parts and an Esp32 for controlling. A RasberryPi is utilized for deteckting artefacts (with the image of the camera) and calculating the way around them.
